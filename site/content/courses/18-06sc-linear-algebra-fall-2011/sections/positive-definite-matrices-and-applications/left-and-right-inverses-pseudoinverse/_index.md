Strang](https://open-learning-course-data-ci.s3.amazonaws.com/18-06sc-linear-algebra-fall-2011/ddd0493392df528ea8b7c3313ba313cf_3_8.jpg) | {{< br >}}{{< br >}}We'd like to be able to "invert _A_" to solve _A_x = b, but _A_ may have only a left inverse or right inverse (or no inverse).
