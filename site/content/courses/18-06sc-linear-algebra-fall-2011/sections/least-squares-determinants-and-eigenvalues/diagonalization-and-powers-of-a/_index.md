Session Overview
----------------

| ![Figure excerpted from 'Introduction to Linear Algebra' by G.S. Strang](https://open-learning-course-data-ci.s3.amazonaws.com/18-06sc-linear-algebra-fall-2011/a9a3121e445d27ee7f5dc1e92968e07e_2_9.jpg) | {{< br >}}{{< br >}}If _A_ has _n_ independent eigenvectors, we can write _A_ = _S_Λ_S_−1, where Λ is a diagonal matrix containing the eigenvalues of _A_. This allows us to easily compute powers of _A_ which in turn allows us to solve difference equations uk+1 = _A_uk.{{< br >}}{{< br >}}
