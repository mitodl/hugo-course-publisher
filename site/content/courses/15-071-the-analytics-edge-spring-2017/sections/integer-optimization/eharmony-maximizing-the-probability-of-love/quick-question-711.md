| ACT\\Pred | 0 | 1 |
| 0 | 1030 | 227 |
| 1 | 126 | 92 

Quick Question
--------------

What is the accuracy of the model, according to the confusion matrix above?

Exercise 1

&nbsp;Numerical Response&nbsp;

Explanation

The accuracy of the model is (1030+92)/(1030+227+126+92) = 0.76
